How it works

Bitsms is not a wallet. Instead, it is an SMS to Bitcoin relay where you sign a transaction in your wallet and text it to this number. Most wallets like Electrum, Bitcoin Core and Armory allow you to sign raw transactions offline. Sending Bitcoin through Bitsms is as secure as sending Bitcoin over the Internet.
